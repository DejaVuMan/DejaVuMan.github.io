Who I am

Student & Programmer based out of Chicago

I'm a student who's currently studying abroad at the University of Warmia and Mazury in Olsztyn! Originally from Chicago, Illinois, I find that I've always been drawn to technology - I remember building my own computer when I was still in elementary school and designing websites with fellow colleagues for clients in highschool, while working at a tax reduction company's IT position. My natural affinity for technology and programming lead me to be an assistant teacher for my computer science class in highschool and gain a strong interest in Python, C++, and Java, from which point I interned with KPMG.

When I'm not actively building and improving my programming toolset, I love to produce videos for my personal YouTube channel, write short stories, and am actively invested in social causes I find important. I had the pleasure of being a Black Lives Matter organizer in Warsaw for a period of time during 2020 and assisted in Casey Smagala's 2019 39th Ward campaign. I always try to give back to my community any way I can and hope that one day I can do just that for the city which helped shape me into who I am today.
